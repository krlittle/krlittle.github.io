After years of running on Jekyll with the Minimal Mistakes theme, I've migrated this site to [Astro](https://astro.build). It's faster to build, simpler to maintain, and I finally have full control over the design without fighting Ruby gem version hell.

The old site was mostly a skeleton anyway — now I'm starting fresh with a setup I actually understand and can iterate on quickly.

## What's here

- **Blog** — where I'll write about software, things I'm cooking, and whatever else is on my mind
- **Projects** — a place to document things I've built or am building

More to come. Stay tuned.
